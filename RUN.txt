🛹 SKATE 4 CLONE — QUICK LAUNCH

One command:
    ./launch.sh

Then:
    - Unity opens
    - Scene auto-generates
    - Play mode activates automatically
    - Skateboard and vibe

Controls:
    W/A/S/D     Steer
    J           Push / Kickflip
    Space       Ollie
    I           Shove-it
    L           Heelflip

Console shows combo counter.

That's it. Have fun. 🛹
